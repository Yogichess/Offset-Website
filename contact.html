Frequently Asked Questions

What are your typical turnaround times?

Turnaround times vary depending on the complexity and volume of your project. Simple projects can be completed in 3-5 business days, while more complex projects may take 7-10 business days. We also offer rush services for urgent requirements.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we have an in-house design team that can help create or refine your artwork. Our designers work closely with you to understand your requirements and deliver designs that align with your brand and objectives.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept a variety of file formats including PDF, AI, PSD, INDD, JPG, and TIFF. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDF files with fonts embedded and images at 300 DPI or higher.

Do you offer shipping services?

Yes, we offer shipping services across India. Shipping costs and delivery times depend on the destination and the weight of your order. We can also arrange for local pickup if you prefer.
