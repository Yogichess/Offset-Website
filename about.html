Our Legacy in Printing

Four decades of excellence in the printing industry

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Offset Printing Press has grown from a small local printing shop to one of the most trusted printing service providers in India. For over four decades, we have been delivering exceptional printing solutions to businesses across various industries.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality printing services with a focus on customer satisfaction. This mission continues to drive us forward as we embrace new technologies and techniques while maintaining our commitment to quality and service excellence.

Today, we serve a diverse client base across India, from small local businesses to large corporations, delivering printing solutions that help them communicate effectively with their audiences and enhance their brand presence.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our four decades of excellence

1985

Founded as a small local printing shop in Sivakasi with a single offset printing press.

1995

Expanded operations with new equipment and technologies, doubling production capacity.

2005

Introduced digital printing services to complement traditional offset printing.

2010

Achieved ISO 9001 certification for quality management systems.

2020

Modernized facility with state-of-the-art equipment and expanded services to include specialized printing techniques.
